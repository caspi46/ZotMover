ZotMover project by Team Mover!!

# Team
Kenny Kim 
Trevor Chow 

# Goal
Building balancing robot with 2-wheels 

# Parts 
- 1 ESP32
- 2 motors: N20
- 1 motor driver: L298N
- 2 18650 batterries
- 2 single 18650 battery holders 
- 1 voltage converter: LM2596 DC-Dc Buck Converter
- 1 gyroscope/accelerometer: SparkFun 6DoF IMU Breakout
